愛天

Love God

Ae-cheon (愛天): Our first motto, to love and attend Heavenly Parent.

愛國

Love Your Nation

Ae-guk (愛國): The second motto, to love and serve one's country.

愛人

Love Humanity

Ae-in (愛人): The final motto, to love all humanity as one's own family.

For 44 years, the Sunhak Institute has stood as the turning point of over 1,500 Blessed Children.

Our mission is to cultivate a deep understanding of Korean heritage through a lens of modern global perspectives. We invite you to explore our archives, engage with our scholars, and join a community dedicated to the pursuit of excellence and the harmony of tradition.
