Curriculum

An Education You Can See, Hear, and Touch

01

Korean Language (한국어)

The curriculum is created to bring students from absolute beginner to the native Korean middle school level, as if they were an actual Korean student. For those interested in continuing their education in Korea, we offer enrollment support for Korean middle school and high school.

You can learn more about studying in Korean middle or high school here

02

General Weekly Schedule

On weekdays, students have class during the morning, freetime in the afternoon, and study time in the evening.

During free time we will take students to the training center, sports field or shopping.

Weekdays students will unwind, sometimes going on trips or simply unwinding at the dorm. We attend church service each Sunday.

03

Monthly Field Trips

Education at Sunhak extends beyond the classroom. Monthly field trips to historical sites allow students to see history firsthand, making the philosophy and tradition come alive.

The Next Step (Optional)

Study at a Local Korean school

Enrollment Support

Moving from a language intensive to a full-time Korean curriculum is a significant milestone. Our dedicated staff provides comprehensive support during the enrollment process for local Middle and High Schools, including:

  • Document and visa preparation for enrollment.
  • 40+ years of experience helping students enroll.
Partner Institution

Sunjung Middle and High School

Located in the Korea's capital, Seoul, the school offers the true local student experience. Students will make Korean friends, live in the big city, and stay in a dormitory with other brothers and sisters.
