Frequently Asked Questions

Academic & Transfers

How do I transfer credits back to my school at home?

The Sunhak Institute provides an official statement that your child spent the past year on our language program. In most cases, students are able to continue on to the next grade, but rarely are students required to repeat a grade.

Do I need prior knowledge of Korean?

No. While knowing Korean helps, all students begin from the basics of learning the Hangeul writing system.

Financial Aid and Tuition

Are scholarships provided?

Yes. Each student, indiscriminately receives a partial scholarship provided by FFWPU International Headquarters. The amount varies each year.
